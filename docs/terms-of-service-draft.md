# Candor Terms of Service Draft

This draft is a starting point for legal review before publishing.

## Use of Candor

Candor is provided as desktop software for recording, transcribing, organizing, and reviewing meeting notes.

## Recording Consent

You are responsible for following the laws and workplace policies that apply to recording meetings in your location. Before recording, get consent from meeting participants when required.

## Local Data

Candor stores meeting data locally on your device and in folders you choose. You are responsible for backing up files you want to keep.

## Third-Party Services

Calendar providers and optional AI services are operated by third parties. Your use of those services is subject to their own terms.

## No Guarantee of Accuracy

Transcripts, summaries, and action items may contain errors. Review important notes before relying on them.
